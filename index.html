About

Why did I create this page?

Someone gave me the idea of publishing all of my artwork here.

What kinds of images do I draw?

Mainly pixel art, but some of my images are absolutely spontaneous
with no specific categorization.

Why do some of my drawings look unsettling?

I like to experiment with new ideas. often times intentionally adding disturbingly bizarre defects to my drawings to ruin the immersion.

When / Why do I draw?

Whenever I feel bored or frustrated.

Do I publish all of my drawings?

No, some of them are too private to share.
